3D Sphere

Personal Project | Front-End Web Development | 3D Modeling | UI/UX Design

It is a personal project that was made keeping in mind anxious and short tempered people. In a world full of stress, something to help control our anxieties and anger is what we all need.

Wacthing many of my close friends and family facing anxiety and stress, I googled for sometime and found out that stress-buster balls are very helpful. This 3D model is an attempt to make something similar.

The way the stress-buster balls work is by keeping our mind concenterated on a major task and making it believing that the event causing us stress and anxiety is secondary task and hence the brain thinks about it in the background of doing the major task that is using the ball.

This gives brain time to relax and calm down and helps come to a logical solution to the problem.

Technologies used: HTML,CSS,SPLINE
